Frogger

2D game
Frogger is an classic arcade game where players control a frog trying to cross a busy highway and a river full of obstacles. The goal is to guide the frog to its home at the top of the screen while avoiding cars, trucks, and falling into the water.
Here I've done the coding and created all of the sprites for the game.
game made in unity, sprites in Aseprite.
